**Project Title**



E-commerce Sales \& Customer Analytics





**Domain**



E-commerce Analytics \& Customer Behavior





**Project Overview**



This project analyzes online retail transaction data to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends. The goal is to generate actionable business insights to improve revenue, customer retention, and product strategy.





**Business Problems \& Questions**



Problem 1: Sales Performance \& Product Insights



**Business Objective:**



Understand sales performance and identify the products driving business growth.



**Business Questions:**



* What is the total revenue generated?
* How has monthly sales changed over time?
* Which countries generate the most revenue?
* What are the top 10 products by revenue?
* Which products contribute to 80% of total revenue (Pareto Analysis)?







**Problem 2: Customer Behavior \& Segmentation**



**Business Objective:**



Understand customer value and identify customer segments for targeted marketing.



**Business Questions:**



* How many unique customers are there, and what is the average revenue per customer?
* Who are the top high-value customers?
* How can customers be segmented using RFM analysis?





**Outcome of the Project**



**This analysis helps businesses:**



* Identify key revenue drivers
* Improve customer retention strategies
* Segment customers for targeted marketing campaigns
* Optimize product performance and inventory planning
